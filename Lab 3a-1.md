This lab guides students through building a full public-facing web presence from the ground up. It covers provisioning a cloud virtual machine, registering and linking a domain name, configuring DNS records, and deploying a website on an Apache web server. From there, students obtain and install a free TLS certificate from Let's Encrypt via Certbot to enable HTTPS. The lab also involves verifying that the certificate is properly installed and that client-server communication is secure. By the end, students will have hands-on experience deploying and managing a publicly accessible, secure web service with complete control over its underlying infrastructure.
